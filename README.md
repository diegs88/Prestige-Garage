# Prestige Garage

## Overview
Prestige Garage is a platform that connects users with a variety of car services. Whether you need repairs, regular maintenance, or detailing, Prestige Garage aims to provide quality services at competitive prices.

## Goals
- To offer a user-friendly interface for booking and managing car services.
- To ensure high-quality and reliable service providers are available.
- To facilitate easy communication between service providers and users.
- To maintain transparency in pricing and service quality.
